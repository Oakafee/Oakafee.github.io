Interpretation of New Jersey

Welcome. Here you will find interpretive information on various New Jersey related topics. The goal of this website is to contribute to our understanding of the Garden State.

I welcome submissions of all kinds on all NJ related topics, to jefrttt (at) gmail.com. To preserve your anonymity, you will be given a unique six-digit Contributor ID, which will appear along with your articles.

The Land

Physiographic, geological, and ecological commentary on the state's natural aspect, with emphasis on the influence natural features have had on NJ's economy, history, and culture. All for the sake of the appreciation and understanding of this much-used, much-abused piece of earth.

We divide NJ into the Appalachian Ridge and Valley provence, the Highlands, the Pidemont (including the Watchung Mountains and Palisades), and the Coastal Plain. See a map of all of them here.

The People

Sketches of the society, politics, and culture of this strange land. I hope to be able to say that this section contains a great diversity of perspectivese, so please contribute!

The Crazy Things People Have Done to the Land

Land use, infrastructure, development and its aftermath. This would include cities and towns, as well as other political divisions of land.
