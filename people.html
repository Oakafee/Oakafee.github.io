NJ's Cultural Regions

Three regions, each with its own personality and social make-up, coexist in NJ, making any attempt at state-wide cultural generaliation dubious. North Jersey natives, like myself, may not possess much South Jersey cultural literacy. For this reason South Jerseyite contributions to this website would be especially valued!.

The designations North, Central and South Jersey are purely cultural in nature. The names carry no legal weight, and the frontiers between them disregard political boundaries. I love this map, created from an NJ.com poll. A detailed comparison of North vs Central vs South can be found within this section.
